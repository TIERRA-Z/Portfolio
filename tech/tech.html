This is the technology page where I will be showcasing all my technology projects and cool technology experiences!

One of my favorite technology experiences in recent years was visiting the Pasco County's Sheriff Office in February 2024 as a part of of a Robotics class field trip at Saint Leo University. At the Pasco County Sheriff's Office, I was able to see how they have integrated robotics into the local sheriff's office. I was able to see the Spot robot dog by Boston Dynamics and drones in action. Enjoy these videos and pictures that I capture from my visit!
